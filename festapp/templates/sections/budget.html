Budget Management

  • Budget for AIKYATH 2026 is planned and managed by the CRS (Class Representatives) team
  • CR's are responsible for collecting money from students in an organized and responsible manner
  • All collected funds are handled with proper transparency and accountability
  • Expenses are planned carefully for events, materials, prizes, and logistics
  • CRS team monitors all spending and avoids unnecessary expenses
  • Maintains proper financial records for every transaction
  • Coordinates with different event teams for smooth budget usage
  • Handles sponsorship funds and additional income sources efficiently
  • Ensures proper balance between income and expenses
  • Overall, CRS ensures every event is conducted successfully within the allocated budget
